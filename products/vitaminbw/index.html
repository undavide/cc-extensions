Vitamin BW

Spice up your Black & White conversions!

BY DAVIDE BARRANCA

Contrast boost

The BW routine features a built-in smart contrast enhancement, that overcomes the issue of flat-looking conversions. A Boost option is available when you need a bit of extra punch and a more 3D look.

BW Filters

Apply the digital equivalent of colored filters (Yellow, Orange, Red, Green, Blue) that Black & White photographers use to mount on camera lenses to exploit the scene contrast: landscapes, portraits, skies, etc.

Toning

Vitamin BW provides a bouquet of toning renderings: besides the default Neutral, you can choose between Warm, Cool or Mixed - an interesting effect which is Warm in the highlights and Cool in the Shadows.

Processing

In order to give you total customization control, Vitamin BW output is split in layers: you can just flatten them if the default result satisfies you, or paint layer masks to precisely target the image to your taste.
