Variants Wizard

When you retouch a Photograph or create a new Design, it is quite common to produce many variations of the same project: generating, sorting, and comparing file duplicates and layer groups is a distracting business when you're deep in the creative process. Variants Manager is a Photoshop CC panel that simplifies and rationalizes this workflow, so that you're free to concentrate on just creating new ideas.

A Truckload of features

Manage multiple versions of a single image. Save Variants permanently or on a temporary Stack. Create a stack from open documents and folders. Save your Stacks. Copy selected layers/groups to a target open document. Compare Variants. Combine selected Variants for blending purpose. Average your Variants. And much more!
